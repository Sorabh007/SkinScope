The Method

Skincare advice built
on chemistry, not vibes.

Most apps give you generic skin-type tips. We run your product's ingredient list against your city's live humidity and AQI — and reason like a formulation chemist.

01
Ingredient fetch
We pull the full INCI list for your product in real time — no manual input, no guessing from marketing copy.
02
Climate context
Your city's monthly average humidity and AQI is mapped. Monsoon Delhi is not the same as dry-winter Delhi.
03
Chemist reasoning
Our AI evaluates Dominant Vehicle → Actives → Synergy → Red Flags. Structured logic, not keyword matching.
04
Climate-twin match
We suggest one alternative from a curated product database — same actives, better vehicle for your climate.

The problem with
skincare advice
right now.

Influencers in LA recommend products tested in 20% humidity. You're in Mumbai in July. It's never going to work the same way.

Heavy creams recommended for oily skin
Because the reviewer is in a dry climate — your 80% humidity turns the same formula into a breakout trigger.
Occlusive serums during pollution spikes
AQI > 150 means PM2.5 sits on product films. Most advice ignores this entirely.
No ingredient-level explanation
"Doesn't suit my skin" is all you get. We tell you which molecule, why, and what to use instead.
